I am a Computer Engineer with the Science Data Processing branch (587) and the Computational and Information Sciences and Technology Office (CISTO) Data Science Group (606.3) at the NASA Goddard Space Flight Center (GSFC). My research focuses on the development of software to support science research in the areas of Earth and Space observation, artificial intelligence, and high-performance computing via cloud and on-premises environments.

Currently, I specialize in hardware accelerated software applications, which include GPU-aided data structures to support large-scale satellite data analysis, deep learning applications to streamline very high-resolution data-driven change detection, foundation models to accelerate science discoveries, and mid-range to sub seasonal natural hazard forecasting which include high resolution lightning and wildfire occurrence modeling. My main goal is to close the gap between science, machine learning, and high performance computing.
